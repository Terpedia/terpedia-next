Site features

What Terpedia offers: feed-first experience, Tersonae, recipes, and more.

  • Activity Feed

    Primary experience: a Facebook-like stream of editorial and user content. Follow Tersonae to shape your feed; filter by type (strains, terpenes, products, Terports, podcasts).

  • Tersona

    Entity Tersonae (e.g. Myrcene, Limonene) subscribe to RSS (PubMed, Google News) per terpene and surface a feed. You can follow Tersonae and chat with each one. User Tersona = your preference profile and recommendations.

  • Post, comment, review

    Post to the feed, comment on posts and entities, and write structured reviews for strains and products. Moderation and reporting included.

  • Encyclopedia

    Core reference: terpene library, strain profiles, essential oils and ingredients, definitions and glossary. Supports the feed and links from feed cards.

  • Terports

    Short, shareable reports on strains, terpenes, or topics. Shareable links and optional PDF; new Terports appear in the feed.

  • Terproduct Database

    Searchable products tied to terpenes and strains. Categories, filters, strain–product links; product updates and reviews surface in the feed.

  • Rx (Recipes)

    Formulations: lists of compounds and/or essential oils with volume or mass. Fork and iterate on recipes; link to Encyclopedia and Terproduct.

  • Podcasts

    Audio education with show notes and cross-links to strains, terpenes, and products. New episodes surface in the feed.

  • Patient records

    Maintain records about a patient: notes, conditions, goals, products or strains used. Ideally stored in cookies or localStorage on the user’s device so data stays local (privacy-first). Export and delete supported.

See FEATURES.md in the repo for the full feature list and backlog.
